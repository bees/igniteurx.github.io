About

UR(x) is an annual conference bringing together university job developers, university recruiting leaders, and student organization leaders. Conference attendees, partners and sponsors all have a vested interest in creating, maintaining, and advancing great university recruiting programs. Join us as we share best practices, discuss challenges, and explore solutions all while establishing great relationships.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the conference about?

UR(x) is is a conference bringing together university job developers, university recruiting leaders, and student organization leaders. Conference attendees, partners and sponsors all have a vested interest in creating, maintaining, or advancing great university recruiting or employer relations programs.

What the heck does "UR(x)" stand for?

UR(x) is a play on the math formula f(x). In our case case, UR = University Recruiting and x is a variable that we use to set the theme for the conference from year to year. This year x = Technical University Recruiting.

Who should attend?

Unversity Job Developers (University Employer Relations, External Relations, Employer Development etc) University Recruiters, Student Organization Leaders, and organizations that help promote or facillitate connections between students and employers.

I will be travelling to the conference, where should I stay?

There are a number of great hotel options in San Francisco and the surrounding area with easy access to both the Caltrain and BART.

How do I get to Lyft Headquarters?

Lyft HQ is conveniently located in the SoMa neighborhood in the city of San Francisco. Lyft is a short walk from the Caltrain Station at 4th & King Street and the BART. Parking is limited. so we adise against driving and suggest you take a Lyft 😉 .

When should I arrive / depart?

Conference registration will begin Tuesday morning at 8AM. The conference will end on Thursday early afternoon by 4pm.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer full refunds for your ticket before March 15th. We will not be able to provide refunds after the 15th of March. You are always welcome to transfer your ticket to someone else, simply let us know and we will process the request.

What meals are provided?

Breakfast, lunch, snacks, and all the coffee you can drink throughout the conference are included with your registration. Please let us know if if you have any special dietary requirements. We are happy to accommodate any dietary requests made in advance of the conference.

Who organizes UR(x)?

UR(x) is organized by a group university administrators and university recruiters with support from great partners, volunteers, and sponsors.
