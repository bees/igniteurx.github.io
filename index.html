The Ignite Conference

A one day conference where the most influential people in tech university recruiting from top engineering colleges, companies and startups will come together. You will connect with movers and shakers at these core schools and learn best practices from top tech interns, former tech recruiters and higher ed administrators at the most selective schools.

About Ignite UR(x)

We create platforms that foster meaningful relationships that are the foundation of any successful university recruiting or employer relations program. We bring together university administrators, university recruiters, and student leaders. Our sole purpose is creating opportunities for talented students, world changing companies, and great institutions of higher learning.
